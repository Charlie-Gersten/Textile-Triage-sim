Participant briefing

Please read the definitions below before starting. In this study, an item is sent to Sorting if it has any contamination or any disruptors. If it has neither, it goes to No Sorting.

Disruptors (examples)

Any item with buttons, zips, trims, or other non-textile materials.

  • Metal/plastic hardware: zips, studs, rivets, snaps, eyelets
  • Non-textile inserts/attachments: hard labels, plastic parts, electronics (if present)
  • Complex assemblies: heavy embellishment, multiple attached materials

Contamination (examples)

Items showing moisture, oils, or large stains. No hazardous/toxic substances are used in this trial.

  • Wet/damp fabric or visible moisture
  • Oil/grease marks
  • Large food/soil stains
